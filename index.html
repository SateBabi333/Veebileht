Yung Lean

Jonatan Aron Leandoer Håstad. Born July 18, 1996, known professionally as Yung Lean, is a Swedish rapper.

Born in Minsk and raised in Stockholm, Yung Lean rose to prominence in 2013 with his song "Ginseng Strip 2002" which went viral on YouTube.
